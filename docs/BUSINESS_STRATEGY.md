# BUSINESS STRATEGY: SUMBANDILA (PHASE 1 - 3)

## 1. Product Direction
**Definition:** Sumbandila is a public digital platform that allows anyone to instantly verify whether an institution or professional is legally registered and accredited in South Africa.

**Core Value Proposition:**
- **Trust:** Instant verification against official sources.
- **Speed:** Mobile-first, QR-based access.
- **Safety:** Preventing fraud in Education and Healthcare.

## 2. Launch Strategy
**Sectors:**
1. **Education (Primary):** Private colleges, Training providers (DHET/QCTO data).
2. **Medical (High Trust):** Doctors, Clinics (HPCSA data).
3. *Phase 2: Legal Professionals*

**Launch Checklist:**
- [x] MVP Build (Android/Web)
- [x] Admin Registry Panel
- [ ] Seed Official Data
- [ ] Google Play Submission
- [ ] Press Release "National Verification Platform Pilot"

## 3. Monetization Model
**Public Users:**
- **FREE:** Unlimited single verifies. Drives mass adoption and brand trust.

**Revenue Streams:**
| Customer Type | Service | Price Model |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Institutions** | "Verified" Badge, Profile Management | R199 - R499/mo |
| **Professionals** | Verified Digital ID, Expiry Alerts | R99/mo |
| **Employers/HR** | Bulk Verification API | R5 - R20 per check |
| **Government** | Custom Integration & Analytics | Annual Contract |

## 4. Government Entry Strategy
**Approach:** "We do not replace you; we make your data accessible to citizens."
**Key Partners:** 
- DHET (Department of Higher Education)
- HPCSA (Health Professions Council)
- SAPS (For fraud reporting integration)

## 5. Legal & IP
- **Disclaimer:** "Verification results are based on submitted and publicly available registration data." (Protects against liability).
- **Patent:** Provisional Patent filed. Goals: Convert to Complete Patent by April 2026.
- **Data Privacy:** POPIA Compliant. Simple "Read-Only" public data access.

## 6. Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** Expo (React Native) - Android + Web.
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express (Microservices ready).
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (Relational integrity for registry).
- **Hosting:** Cloud-ready (Docker/Render).
